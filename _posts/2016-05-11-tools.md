#### OSX El Capitan with Sublime Text 3

    ln -s /Applications/Sublime\ Text.app/Contents/SharedSupport/bin/subl /usr/local/bin/subl
    ➜  ~ vi ~/.bash_profile
    export PATH=$PATH:~/usr/local/bin
    ➜  ~ source ~/.bash_profile
    ➜  ~ subl

  it works fine!
